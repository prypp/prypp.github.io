A bit about me

The detail-obsessed one a Japanese team can lean on by day — and the maker of soft little characters by night.

I spent three years running reception and administration at a Japanese clinic in Bangkok, then made a deliberate move to Japan for four years — reaching business-level Japanese (JLPT N2) and first-hand cultural fluency while working customer-facing retail roles. Back in Thailand, I now freelance as a Japanese–Thai translator and lean on AI tools (Claude, Codex) every day — this very site is hand-built that way. On the side I run ra-yeah! and am writing Japanese & English learning books (still in progress).
