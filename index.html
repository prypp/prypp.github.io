002 — About

The hand behind it

I'm a digital artist who paints with light and codes the tools that make the painting faster. I care about warmth, texture, and getting the small details exactly right.

My work lives between the canvas and the command line — illustration and concept art on a tablet, and Python tooling for everything around it (brush pipelines, batch processing, generative studies). This portfolio itself is hand-built and open-source.
